JUN 04

My Go-To Saturday: Skin Routine

Saturday is a day that most of us get a chance to breathe. The work week is over, the alarm clock does NOT go off, and the sun shines a little brighter. On Saturday I get to pamper my skin a little when I start my day. Normally I sign-up for a spin class, but I usually will start of with mini-facial. Here is what I use:

  • Cotton pad with toner
  • 3 Second Starter
  • Sheet Mask (20 minutes)

After removing the sheet mask I pat everything in add some translucent powder and I'm off to Spin Class! I sweat it out for about an hour and head home for my second pamper session. Because I feel that exercise makes so much of a difference in my skin I get a huge rush out of sweating. Some of the gunk from the week is now at the surface. Drinking water is my first go to and I wipe my face down with a makeup wipe then I pull out my next set of products:

  • Deep Clean Pore Glacial Clay (20 Minutes)
  • Shower and wash up...then back to spa treatment
  • Collegen Impact Essential Mask (20 Minutes)
  • Innisfree Orchid Eye Cream
  • The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%(for the acne)
  • [Cosrx] Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence

As if my face couldn't be more moisturized at the very end I added a little more. Sometimes I replace that step with sunscreen if I will be outside. Well that's it...if I haven't eaten anything by the end of all of that I head to kitchen and turn on some Netflix to begin my chill routine.
