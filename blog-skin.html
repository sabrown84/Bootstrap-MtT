SkinCare

There is nothing better than taking care of your body.

It Always Feels Good to be Clean

This korean cleanser is all encompassing. It's so good that it takes off everything. I use it in the evening to get off all my makeup, sunscreen, and any dirt from the day. My face becomes a clean slate again and ready to absorb the rest of my night time routine.

  • Beyond Phytoganic Facial Foam

I use a cleansing water in the morning as a gentle wash before my morning routine, but we can talk about that later. At night the time to relax starts with the Beyond facial foam and I'm on my way to dream town. It doesn't dry my skin out at all and then the rest of my steps begin. Its one of my favorite experiences.

Fall in Love with Masks

For me masks came into my life when it was time to give up on my skin. When I started trying sheet masks I couldn't stop myself. These are some of the best things that I have tried by far. I like clay masks, but I have really come to love sheet masks. I don't have a box full like I have seen, but I do have a few that have been great:

  • Innisfree It's Real Squeeze Mask Sheet
  • Hada Labo Tokyo Anti-Aging Facial Sheet Mask
  • TONYMOLY I'm Real Mask Sheet

That's just to name a few. There are so many out there. I always find that my skin looks and feels more amazing the more I do this. It's kind of like when you deep condition your hair except this is for your face.

Skin Temperature Matters!

I try not to spend too much time in the sun, but sometimes you just can't help but soak some in. If you are like me and you were born with a little melanin and are semi-native to an island in the caribbean then, you love to be HOT. 'Tis the season to be aware of the sun. Have your sunscreen ready and your beauty mist on blast. I tend to reach for some of my favorites on this one, but there are so many out there I also have some on my wish list. Check out my list:

  • Innisfree Aloe Revital Skin Mist
  • Snail White Syn-ake Mist
  • SKINFOOD Aqua Grape Bounce Mist
  • Pyunkang Yul Mist Toner
  • MISSHA All Around Safe Block Essence Sun Milk
  • Missha All Around Safe Block Soft Finish Sun Milk
  • Missha All Around Safe Block Waterproof Sun Milk

As you can see in these hot months my beach bag consists of all sorts of aloe, cooling mists, and Missha sun milks. I know that its bad when the skin's temperature gets too high...causing a change in the skin barrier. Therefore, I do my best to have some back-up moisture and protection when its time to spend time in the sun. I recommend you do the same. Bring on the fun!

  • Vitamin C Serum and Hyaluronic Acid
  • Cosrx Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence
  • MISSHA All Around Safe Block Essence Sun Milk
  • Mizon Water Volume Ex First Essence
  • The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%

When I'm surfing the interwebs for new products I try to pay attention to what my skin needs. I also love BHA for my acne and vitamin C for my scars. This vitamin C serum (AM routine) in particular makes me happy because it doesn't burn or sting and it has hyaluronic acid in it...I live in Atlanta, GA which is hot and humid, so no worries. *Be careful with hyaluronic acids if you live in a dry climate. My Go-To Saturday: Skin Routine

Saturday is a day that most of us get a chance to breathe. The work week is over, the alarm clock does NOT go off, and the sun shines a little brighter. On Saturday I get to pamper my skin a little when I start my day. Normally I sign-up for a spin class, but I usually will start off with a mini-facial. Here is what I use:

  • Cotton pad with toner
  • 3 Second Starter
  • Sheet Mask (20 minutes)

After removing the sheet mask I pat everything in add some translucent powder and I'm off to Spin Class! I sweat it out for about an hour and head home for my second pamper session. Because I feel that exercise makes so much of a difference in my skin I know that I get to treat myself once I get home...besides the fact that I get a huge rush out of sweating. Some of the gunk from the week is now at the surface. Drinking water is my first go to and I wipe my face down with a makeup wipe then I pull out my next set of products:

  • Deep Clean Pore Glacial Clay (20 Minutes)
  • Shower and wash up...then back to spa treatment
  • Collegen Impact Essential Mask (20 Minutes)
  • Innisfree Orchid Eye Cream
  • The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc 1%(for the acne)
  • [Cosrx] Advanced Snail 96 Mucin Power Essence

As if my face couldn't be more moisturized at the very end I add a little more. Sometimes I replace that step with sunscreen if I will be outside. Well that's it...if I haven't eaten anything by the end of all of that I head to kitchen and turn on some Netflix to start my chill time.
