I'm Doing This to Solve Puzzles

I used to do a lot of puzzles when I was young. I loved it...I still do. They take a few hours to complete a 1000 piece, or a 5000 piece. For me it was about how the pieces fit together not just about the big picture. Thinking it though was the best part. I think that's why I got into code/programming/software. I started to get obsessed pretty late in life, but once it started it hasn't stopped...it's been a few years now. One of the ost valuable things that I have learned is to follow your obsessions.

I getting into doing oopen source work now. It pretty amazing the things that people have come up with out there. It's evevn more amazing that they are allowing people like me to be a part of it. Its pretty fun and I'm learning so much about how others work. The infrastructure is fascinating. Looking into someone else code and finding a place for your code to live is so so cool. I'm also learning about new languages and how they work together...who knew. It can all be overwhelming and intimidating, but I'm too excited to give into my emotions right now. Keep learning and keep doing.
